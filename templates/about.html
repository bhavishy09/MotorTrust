About This Project

🚗 Car Price Prediction Engine

This web application leverages advanced machine learning to predict the selling price of used cars based on critical market features. Our goal is to provide car owners, buyers, and dealerships with lightning-fast, data-driven price estimates to make informed financial decisions.

The prediction core isolates key valuation factors:

  • Year of Manuf. / Current Age
  • Showroom Baseline Price
  • Total Kilometers Driven
  • Fuel Type Efficiency
  • Seller Category
  • Transmission Config
