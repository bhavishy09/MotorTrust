About This Project

🚗 Car Price Prediction Web App

This web application leverages machine learning to predict the selling price of used cars based on various features such as age, mileage, fuel type, and transmission. Our goal is to provide car owners and buyers with accurate price estimates to make informed decisions.

The prediction model is trained on real-world car data and considers multiple factors including:

  • Year of manufacture and current age
  • Current showroom price of the model
  • Total kilometers driven
  • Fuel type (Petrol, Diesel, CNG)
  • Seller type (Dealer vs Individual)
  • Transmission type (Manual vs Automatic)
